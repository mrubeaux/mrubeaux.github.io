Welcome! I am an associate professor in the [IMPACT](https://ltsi.univ-rennes.fr/IMPACT_home) team, [LTSI](https://ltsi.univ-rennes.fr), [Univ Rennes](https://www.univ-rennes.fr/en), France. For more details on my background and current research projects, you can have a look at my [CV](https://mrubeaux.github.io/cv/). A complete list of my publications is also available [here](https://mrubeaux.github.io/publications/).

I earned my PhD in Image and Signal Processing and Telecommunications as well as a Master degree from the [Université de Rennes](https://www.univ-rennes.fr/en), and a Engineering degree in Electronics from [Brest National School of Engineering](https://www.enib.fr/en_enib/).

My research focuses on medical image processing applied to oncology.
